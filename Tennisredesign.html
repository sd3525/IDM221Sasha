Tennis.com Redesign

Overview:

Tennis.com is the Tennis Channelâ€™s official website. It helps fans stay on top of tennis news like scores, rankings, tv scheduling, and more.

Problem Statement

The goal of my redesign was to help Tennis Channel earn more profit by keeping and gaining more customers on their website through creating a more intuitive design which would help the user accomplish their goals in a simple manner.

The User:

This website is geared towards tennis fans of all ages, who want to learn more about players, tournaments, and rankings. It also not only caters to users who are subscribed to the tv channel package, but also to users who just want to stay up to date with the tennis world.

Process:

1. Understanding the problems with Tennis.com website:

I used a heuristic analysis chart to pinpoint the problem areas of the website. Navigation, task orientation, and organization/credibility were the main issues.

Navigation: top menu bar is cut off

Task Orientation: Video stays at the bottom right of the screen and obstructs the view of articles and possible important information

Organization/credibility: important articles are mixed in with insignificant articles on the home page. Some articles are not even related to tennis
